Certificate Verification

Certificates issued by the UNEP Copenhagen Climate Centre (UNEP‑CCC) include a unique Certificate ID that can be used to verify their authenticity.

Online certificate verification is currently under development. Certificates issued during this period remain fully valid and verifiable.

How to Verify a Certificate

  1. Locate the Certificate ID printed on the certificate.
  2. Send the Certificate ID to: aristeidis.tsakiris@un.org
Online verification (coming soon)

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is online verification not yet available? Online verification will be enabled once all technical systems are approved for external access in accordance with UN IT security policies.
Are certificates issued before online verification valid? Yes. All certificates are issued from a centrally managed registry and are valid irrespective of when online verification becomes available.
Will I need a new certificate later? No. Certificates already issued will become verifiable online using the same Certificate ID.

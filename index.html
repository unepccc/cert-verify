Certificate Verification

Certificates displayed or distributed by the UNEP Copenhagen Climate Centre (UNEP‑CCC) include a unique Certificate ID that can be used to verify their authenticity.

Important:
Online, automated certificate verification is currently under development. Certificates issued during this period remain fully valid and verifiable using the process described below.

How to Verify a Certificate

To verify the authenticity of a certificate:

  1. Locate the Certificate ID printed on the certificate.
  2. Send the Certificate ID to:
    aristeidis.tsakiris@un.org

Verification will be confirmed by the UNEP Copenhagen Climate Centre.

Future Online Verification

Online verification using the same Certificate ID will be made available once the relevant verification services are enabled. Certificates already issued will become verifiable online without any need for re‑issuance or replacement.
